About us

ORFLAX India Pvt Ltd is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality electric wires and cables, backed by over 15 years of experience. Operating under the trusted brand name ORFLAX, we specialize in copper and aluminum cables that meet IS specifications and international standards, tailored to various electrical applications. Headquartered in North India, our ISO 9001:2015 certification reflects our commitment to quality, ensuring reliable and safe products. Whether for industrial complexes or residential buildings, ORFLAX is dedicated to meeting your unique electrical needs.
